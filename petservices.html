Grooming

Full-Service Grooming

Our full-service grooming includes a luxurious bath, thorough brushing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and a stylish haircut tailored to your dog's breed or your preferences. We use high-quality, pet-friendly products to ensure your dog looks and feels their best.

Spa Day for Pups

Treat your pup to a spa day with our pampering services. This package includes a relaxing bath with specialty shampoos, a gentle massage, teeth brushing, and a pawdicure. Your dog will leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Express Grooming

For busy pet parents, our express grooming service provides a quick and efficient grooming experience. Your dog will receive a speedy bath, light trimming, and essential grooming services to maintain their coat and hygiene in no time.

Specialty Breed Styling

We specialize in breed-specific styling to enhance the natural beauty of your dog's coat. Whether it's a classic poodle trim, a stylish schnauzer cut, or any other breed-specific style, our skilled groomers will create a look that complements your dog's unique features.

Soothing Skin Treatments

If your dog has sensitive skin or allergies, our soothing skin treatments are the perfect solution. We use hypoallergenic products and gentle techniques to provide relief for irritated skin, leaving your dog feeling comfortable and happy.

Seasonal Grooming Packages

Celebrate each season with our themed grooming packages. From festive holiday trims to summer spa sessions, our seasonal grooming options add a touch of flair to your dog's appearance. Check our calendar for upcoming seasonal specials!

Pet Day Camp

Day Camp 1

Welcome to Pet Day Camp 1!

Where furry friends embark on exciting adventures and make lasting memories!

Morning Welcome:

The day kicks off with a warm welcome and check-in, where our dedicated staff ensures that each pet feels comfortable and ready for a day of fun. We understand that every pet is unique, so we take the time to learn about their preferences and special needs.

Playtime Extravaganza:

Our spacious play areas are perfect for energetic romps and playful interactions. Supervised by our trained caregivers, pets engage in games that stimulate both their minds and bodies. From fetch to hide-and-seek, our playtime extravaganza ensures that your pet stays active and social throughout the day.

Enriching Workshops:

At Pet Day Camp 1, we believe in holistic pet care. That's why we offer enriching workshops that cater to different aspects of your pet's well-being. Whether it's a grooming tutorial, basic training sessions, or even pet-friendly arts and crafts, our workshops provide valuable experiences for your furry friends.

Pampering Sessions:

Everyone deserves a bit of pampering, and our pets are no exception! Our pampering sessions include gentle grooming, massages, and relaxation time to ensure that your pet feels refreshed and loved during their day at camp.

Interactive Feeding:

We understand the importance of a balanced diet for our pets. During mealtime, our caregivers engage in interactive feeding, providing mental stimulation as pets work for their treats or meals through puzzle feeders and other engaging techniques.

Outdoor Adventures:

Weather permitting, our camp includes exciting outdoor adventures. Whether it's a nature walk, a splash in the kiddie pool, or a game of agility, we make sure that your pet enjoys the great outdoors in a secure and monitored environment.

Quiet Time and Cuddles:

After a day filled with activities, we provide a cozy and calm environment for pets to unwind. Soft blankets, soothing music, and plenty of cuddles ensure that every pet has a chance to relax and recharge.

Daily Report Card:

Before you pick up your pet, you'll receive a personalized daily report card outlining the day's activities, behavior, and any special moments your furry friend experienced. We believe in transparency and want you to feel confident that your pet had the best day possible.

Day Camp 2

Welcome to Pet Day Camp 2!

Another day filled with exciting activities awaits your furry friends!

Morning Greetings:

Day 2 starts with cheerful greetings as our staff welcomes each pet with enthusiasm. We ensure a positive and energetic atmosphere to kickstart another day of fun and adventures.

Interactive Playtime:

Our interactive playtime sessions continue with new games and toys. Pets engage in mentally stimulating activities that keep them entertained and encourage social interaction with fellow campers.

Training and Agility:

Day 2 includes specialized training sessions and agility exercises. Our professional trainers work with pets on obedience and introduce them to agility equipment, fostering mental sharpness and physical fitness.

Arts and Crafts:

Creativity takes center stage as pets participate in arts and crafts activities. From paw paintings to personalized pet tags, our arts and crafts sessions allow pets to express themselves in unique ways.

Snack Time Social:

Midday brings a social snack time where pets can enjoy healthy treats and refreshments. It's also an opportunity for them to socialize with their fellow campers in a relaxed and friendly environment.

Nap and Relaxation:

After a morning filled with excitement, pets have dedicated nap and relaxation time. Cozy beds and calming music create a peaceful environment, ensuring that every pet gets the rest they need.

Creative Photo Shoot:

Professional photographers capture adorable moments during our creative photo shoot. Pet parents receive digital copies of these memorable snapshots, providing a lasting memory of their pet's day at camp.

Farewell and Goodie Bags:

As the day comes to an end, each pet receives a farewell hug and a goodie bag filled with treats, toys, and a summary of their day. It's our way of saying thank you for being part of Pet Day Camp 2!
